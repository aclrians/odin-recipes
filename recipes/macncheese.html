Mac n' Cheese

Description

One of the most classic meals of all time. A true American staple that
is bound to please the wealthy and the poor.

Ingredients Needed :

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt , Pepper , Paprika
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps to Follow

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over msacaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
